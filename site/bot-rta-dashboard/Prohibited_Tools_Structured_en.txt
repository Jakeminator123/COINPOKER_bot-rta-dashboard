PROHIBITED TOOLS – OVERVIEW (2025)
===================================

Purpose
-------
This document consolidates and structures tools and services that are not allowed to be used during play.

Core rule during play
---------------------
• All real-time assistance (RTA) and solver usage during an active hand is prohibited.
• All automated play software (bots) is prohibited.
• All unauthorized collection/sharing of hand histories is prohibited.
• All dynamic HUDs or screen‑scraping tools that read live tables are prohibited.

Categories & lists
==================

1) DATA MINING / HAND HISTORY SHARING
-------------------------------------
("Databases or sites that provide hands you did not play yourself")

- HandHQ — nothing reliable to find online (appears defunct)
- HHDealer — currently selling 1.46M of our hands
- PokerTableRatings (PTR) — nothing reliable to find online (appears defunct)
- PokerStove Databases (public/mined versions) — cannot be blocked
- DriveHUD Cloud Sharing (unauthorized data pools) — DriveHUD is permitted after we confirm dynamic HUD modes are disabled
- SharkScope Hand History Imports (live collection) — cannot be blocked
- OmahaTracker Data Service — nothing reliable to find online
- All custom APIs/marketplaces for HH scraping
- SmartHand — seems to only have Stars & 888 hands
- StatName — does not have CP hands

2) AUTO-SEATING / TABLE SELECTION SCRIPTS
-----------------------------------------
("Programs that automatically seat you at tables based on stats/profiles")

- SeatMojo — appears discontinued; replaced by TimeMojo (Stars & FullTilt)
- StarsCaption (if seat scripting is enabled) — not for CP
- PartyCaption (seat scripts) — not for CP
- iPoker Tools (auto-seat modes) — not for CP
- SharkyStrator — not for CP
- TableScan Turbo (live auto-join versions) — not for CP
- SeatScript — nothing reliable online / StarsHelper seat finder — not for CP

5) DYNAMIC HUDS / SCREEN‑SCRAPING TOOLS
---------------------------------------
- Hand2Note (dynamic or real‑time mode)

- PokerTracker/HM – proprietary live plug‑ins that update mid‑hand
- Poker Edge, Holdem Indicator, Tournament Shark
- SmartBuddy, Poker Crusher, Poker Office
- ZyngaHUD / generic OCR HUDs (screen‑recording/screen‑capture‑based)

Policy notes
------------
• “Not for CP” means the tool/script is not permitted for use on CP.
• “Cannot be blocked” describes a technical limitation in client/platform blocking — the prohibition still applies.
• Website logins listed are intended to facilitate detection/blocking and do not constitute permission to use the service.
